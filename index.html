Method

I build from atmosphere and structure at the same time.

A site or product has to feel memorable, but it also has to orient people. I look for the path through the information first, then shape the visual language around it.

01

Observe

Look at the material, context and friction before deciding what the product or site should become.

02

Structure

Organise the information, hierarchy and path so people know where they are going.

03

Atmosphere

Shape the visual rhythm, mood and sense of entry around the structure.

04

Release

Refine, test and make the experience clear enough to be used and remembered.
